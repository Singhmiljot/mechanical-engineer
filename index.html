About me

As a Mechanical Design Engineer, I focus on creating innovative solutions for complex challenges, with a specialisation in CAD, FEA, and product development. My expertise extends across industries such as manufacturing and nuclear technology, where I design electro-mechanical systems and develop bespoke machinery for hazardous environments. I hold a strong belief in a human-centred approach to engineering, ensuring that the end-user's needs are always at the forefront of my designs. I am passionate about advancing the field of engineering, constantly learning new skills and exploring side projects to stay at the cutting edge of technology. My experience spans both academia and industry, where I’ve worked on individual and team-based projects, solving diverse and complex problems. My focus lies in remote handling systems, precision tooling, and automated robotics, which allows me to create efficient, sustainable systems that have a real-world impact. I invite you to explore my portfolio to learn more about my projects and experience. Please don’t hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or would like to discuss potential collaborations.

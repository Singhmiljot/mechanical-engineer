Recreating the Classic: Mercedes-Benz SL 300 Design in Fusion 360

This project was a passion-driven recreation of the iconic Mercedes-Benz SL 300 using Fusion 360. The goal was to precisely capture the intricate details of the classic car's design, including its sleek bodywork, aerodynamics, and mechanical features.

Leveraging advanced CAD techniques, the project emphasised both aesthetic accuracy and structural integrity, paying homage to one of the most celebrated automotive designs in history.

By modelling the car's frame and components, this project showcased an expert blend of automotive design knowledge and mechanical CAD skills, reflecting the elegance and engineering brilliance of the original SL 300.

Skills Utilised:

  • Advanced CAD Modelling
  • Automotive Design
  • Surface Modelling
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • 3D Visualisation and Rendering

Software Used:

  • Fusion 360 (3D Modelling & Surface Design)
  • Autodesk Inventor (Design Validation)
  • Adobe Photoshop (Visual Enhancements for Rendering)
  • Microsoft Excel (Bill of Materials & Design Calculations)

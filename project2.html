Prospects of Utilising Carbon Fibre in Public Transport Systems

This project focused on the integration of carbon fibre components into public transportation systems in the United Kingdom. The goal was to explore the potential of carbon fibre's lightweight and high-strength properties to improve fuel efficiency and reduce material wear in transport vehicles. Using SolidWorks and ANSYS, carbon fibre components were designed and optimised for performance under operational loads, including stress, strain, and fatigue analysis.

The project highlighted the sustainability benefits of carbon fibre, such as reduced fuel consumption and lower maintenance costs, making it a promising material for the future of public transportation.
